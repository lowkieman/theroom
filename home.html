what are legacy points?

Legacy Points is the loyalty and rewards programme of the ALX Fellowship. As you participate and contribute to the ALX Community earn points that can be redeemed for exciting perks and benefits.

Save your points to get the rewards you want and 'spend' your points when you're ready to be rewarded for your hard work! Once you redeem a reward the points used will be deducted from your total, so don't forget to stay engaged with the community in order to keep earning!

How can I collect more points?

For All Members:
  • 250 pts: Update your employment status every 90 days by filling out this form
  • 250 pts: Upload your CV to your profile
  • 250 pts: Host an event online or in-person (per event)
  • 150 pts: Onboard to The Portal (eligible for 1-time redemption)
  • 150 pts: Added Pitch Video to Profile
  • 150 pts: Added LinkedIn URL to Profile
  • 25 pts: Jobs applied for via Futurelist
  • 25 pts: Attend an In-Person Event (per event)
  • 10 pts: Attend a Virtual Event (per event - must be in attendance for minimum 30 mins)
  • 10 pts: Post in the Community (max 10 redemptions per month)
  • 5 pts: Comment on post in The Community (max 20 redemptions per month)
For ALX Learners only:
  • 250 pts:Complete your ALX Programme (eligible for 1-time redemption)
  • 100 pts:Submission of first milestone (eligible for 1-time redemption)
  • 50 pts:For completing each milestone from milestone 2
Please note that points can take up to 2 weeks to be reflected on your profile

Keep an eye out for more ways to redeem points!

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What are Legacy Points?

Legacy Points are reward points you accumulate as an active subscriber. They can be redeemed for even more exclusive perks and experiences.

How do I redeem my benefits?

Once you're subscribed, you'll access your personalized Legacy Card benefits dashboard. From there, you can browse, select, and enjoy the benefits that resonate with you.

How do I redeem my Legacy Points for rewards?

Redeeming your Legacy Points is easy! Simply visit our Rewards Center, where you can explore a variety of exciting rewards and perks available exclusively to Legacy Card subscribers. Choose the rewards that resonate with you, and use your accumulated Legacy Points to unlock them.

How can I get help or support for any issues?

Our dedicated support team is available to assist you. Simply reach out via email or phone, and we'll be happy to address any questions or concerns you may have.
